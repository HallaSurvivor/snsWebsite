Tisbert Sketch Comedy

To get involved, join the Facebook page or email whatistisbert@gmail.com

Are you a fan of SNL? Do you want to learn how to write like Tina Fey? Do you like laughter and friendship? Then come join Tisbert, Carnegie Mellon’s premier sketch comedy troupe! A subtroupe of Scotch’n’Soda Theatre, Tisbert provides opportunities for students across campus to create and perform original sketch comedy, cumulating in a brand new sketch show every semester. With weekly writing meetings open to all Carnegie Mellon students and a new workshop series aimed at developing sketch-writing skills, Tisbert is a great place for writers of every skill level. It doesn’t matter if you’ve never written a sketch or if you’ve spent your entire life coming up with beat lines - if you like comedy, Tisbert is the place for you!


The No Parking Players

More information at thenoparkingplayers.com

NPP is Carnegie Mellon's most awesome (and only) improv theatre troupe! Whether you've had a lot of experience with improv or are just getting interested, NPP Workshops are a great way to improve your skills and have a lot of fun. In addition to workshops, our performance group, an audition based group within NPP, performs long form shows about twice a month in the blackbox theater on campus. You can drop into any of our workshops at any time, and you can even enroll in our StuCo, 98-128 Fundamentals Of Improv Comedy and get 3 units of credit!


Workshopping

To get involved, join the Facebook page

Throughout Scotch’n’Soda’s early years, shows were performed, managed, and written entirely by students. Today, Workshopping embraces S’n’S’s origins by facilitating the creation of original student works. Workshopping hosts bimonthly lectures to build a ‘toolkit’ of skills for newbie writers, in addition to office hours to chat freely about projects and ideas. This sub-troupe also hosts staged readings of new plays and musicals, as well as oldies written by S’n’S alumni. We welcome writers of all experience levels! Find us on Facebook @snsworkshopping.
